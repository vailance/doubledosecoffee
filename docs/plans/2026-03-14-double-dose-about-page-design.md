# Double Dose Coffee About Page Design

**Goal:** Rebuild the About page as an editorial founder story that feels personal, specific, and aligned with the cinematic homepage direction.

## Story Direction

The About page should move away from generic brand copy and feel like a guided introduction to why Double Dose exists. The approved narrative is founder-led and anchored in a fascination with how origin and roast completely change the coffee experience. That curiosity should expand into two related ideas: coffee as warm hospitality and Double Dose as a modern coffee identity in Melaka.

The tone should be polished but human. It should sound like a person with taste and conviction, not a generic brand template.

## Page Structure

### Hero

- Replace the plain "About Us" headline with a more authored statement.
- Keep atmospheric photography and cinematic motion, but make the copy more intimate.
- Introduce the founder point of view immediately.

### Origin Chapter

- Explain that Double Dose began from an obsession with how beans from different origins and roast profiles create different experiences in the cup.
- Keep the writing specific and sensory rather than corporate.

### Philosophy Chapter

- Show the brand belief that great coffee should be both thoughtful and approachable.
- Blend craft and hospitality instead of presenting them as separate ideas.

### Melaka / Experience Chapter

- Position Double Dose as a modern coffee identity in Melaka.
- Explain what guests should feel when they visit: welcomed, curious, and un-intimidated.

### Closing CTA

- End with a warm invitation to visit the space or explore the beans.

## Visual Direction

- Reuse the homepage editorial motion language so the site feels cohesive.
- Use chapter-based layouts with strong type, warm gradients, and restrained reveal animations.
- Favor layered cards, dividers, and image moments over plain text blocks.

## Accessibility and Motion

- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Keep reveals calm and intentional, not constant or distracting.
- Preserve strong contrast in light sections and on photography overlays.

## Files Expected To Change

- `src/app/about/page.tsx`
- `src/components/about/*`
- `src/app/globals.css`
- `docs/plans/*`

## Notes

This page should avoid invented founder history, sourcing claims, or operational details that were not provided by the user.
